Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Registration

How do I create an account?

Click the "Register" button on our website, fill in your details, and verify your email address. The process takes less than 5 minutes.

Is KYC verification mandatory?

Yes, KYC verification is required to enable withdrawals and access all platform features. This helps us maintain security and compliance.

Can I have multiple accounts?

No, each individual is allowed only one account. Multiple accounts may lead to suspension.

Investments & Returns

How are returns calculated?

Returns are based on our expert trading in cryptocurrency markets. Each plan has fixed returns based on the investment amount and duration.

When do I receive my returns?

Returns are paid at the end of the maturity period for each investment plan (3-6 weeks depending on the plan).

Can I withdraw my investment early?

Investments are locked until maturity to ensure optimal returns. Early withdrawal is not permitted.

Deposits & Withdrawals

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept MTN Mobile Money, Telecel Cash, Bitcoin, and TRON (TRC20) for deposits and withdrawals.

How long do withdrawals take?

Withdrawals are typically processed within 24 hours after approval. Crypto withdrawals may take additional time for blockchain confirmations.

Are there any fees?

We don't charge deposit fees. Withdrawal fees depend on the payment method and are clearly displayed before confirmation.

Referral Program

How does the referral program work?

You earn GHS 24 for each friend who signs up with your link, plus 4% of their initial investment amount.

When do I receive referral bonuses?

Referral bonuses are credited instantly when your referred friend makes their first investment.

Is there a limit to referral earnings?

No, you can refer unlimited friends and earn unlimited referral bonuses.
